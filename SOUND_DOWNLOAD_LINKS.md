# Quick Sound Download Links

Copy these URLs into your browser to find and download free cat sounds:

## Pixabay (Recommended - Easiest)

### Meow Sounds
1. https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/cat%20meow/?duration=0-30
2. https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/kitten%20meow/
3. https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/cat%20calling/

### Purr Sounds
1. https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/cat%20purr/
2. https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/cat%20purring/
3. https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/purr/

**Instructions:**
- Click each link
- Click the download button on sounds you like
- Download at least 10 meows and 10 purrs
- Save to a folder like `~/Downloads/cat_sounds/`

## Alternative: YouTube to MP3 (For Personal Use)

If you find good cat sound compilations on YouTube:

1. Use a YouTube to MP3 converter (for personal use only)
   - https://ytmp3.cc
   - https://y2mate.com

2. Download the audio

3. Use Audacity to split into individual sounds

4. Save as meow1.mp3, meow2.mp3, etc.

**Note:** Only use this for personal projects, not commercial distribution.

## What Makes a Good Sound

### Meows
- **Duration**: 0.5 - 2 seconds
- **Quality**: Clear, minimal background noise
- **Variety**: Mix of high/low pitch, loud/soft, different emotions
- **Examples**: 
  - Short chirp
  - Demanding "feed me" meow
  - Questioning "meow?"
  - Excited trill
  - Gentle greeting

### Purrs
- **Duration**: 2 - 4 seconds
- **Quality**: Smooth, continuous
- **Variety**: Different volumes and rumble depths
- **Examples**:
  - Soft content purr
  - Loud happy purr
  - Deep rumbling purr
  - Motor-like continuous purr
